Silk flowers for weddings and more 

We love to design with silk flowers for all occasions. We use only the highest quality botanically correct silk flowers to  provide you with fabulous and unique floral designs  which you can use and re-purpose long  after your event is finished. Silk flowers make wonderful memories, home decorations and inspired gifts. They can be resold or taken apart and used for other artistic uses later on.
